This website basically introduces the concepts of "social media" and "we media" and their relationships. Social media and we media are two types of new medias, which are developing rapidly in the last decade. This website also discovers why these media can attract users naturally and tightly.

Terms "Fragmentation of Time" and "Six Degrees of Separation" are also introduced as the basis of social media and we media theory. Besides, "Adhesiveness of User" is another important factor that determines if a social media can survive and survive in a dynamic way.

After the analysis, there are 4 pages introducing 4 fields of platforms using these medias. In each field, two or more typical sample platforms are given to explain how the media could function in real life. After viewing the concepts and the sample platforms, I believe you will have a clearer idea about these interesting and rising medias!
